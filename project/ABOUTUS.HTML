About Us

SUNCROSS BIKES is promoted by Naren International, Ludhiana (A Govt. of India Recognized Export House) to cater to the needs of Indian Bicycle Lovers in the MTB segment with its Premium Quality Bicycles.

Suncross Bikes specializes in Hard Tail MTB's, Full Suspension Bikes, Hybrid Bikes, DTB's, City MTB Bikes and Trekking series along with one of the Best MTB Bikes in Kid Segment too and has already become a market leader since its first introduction of Bikes since 2004 with its foray in Indian Market.

The Company is promoted by Mr. Rajesh Kalra engaged in serving the bicycle industry internationally since last two decades. The Company is also engaged actively in Exports of complete range of Bicycles & Bicycle Parts in almost 30 countries worldwide under their popular brand names of SUNCROSS, CICLOSPORT, BICISPORT.

The Company and its promoter enjoys high credibility and trust of its clients and believes in firm commitment based on Quality, Higher Efficiency, pursuing better innovative products and of course gaining "Customers Satisfaction". The Company is keen to cater to the needs of its Clients for whom Quality is most important and to serve the required expectations with its world class products.

We at SUNCROSS BIKES have a very clear vision and working policy based on "Serve and Performance". Through our highly qualified and talented Engineering Team who take stringent control of all its products, improvised R&D, Testing techniques as per EN-14766 (European Standard for MTB Bikes) and EN-14764 (European Standard for City and Trekking Bikes) and other international standards, we have further understood from our past experience and working culture that "Quality products now pursue the customer in purchasing decisions".

The Company is further having its active base in Far-East (P.R of China) and we also catering to our Clients requirements with Chinese/Taiwanese produced Bicycle Parts, Components, Tyres & Tubes & Accessories too on most competitive terms.

While thanking your good self for taking your time to access our products/webpage, we SUNCROSS BIKES hereby extend our hand of friendship and commitment to become Business Partners and lets endeavor to build a strong and healthy relationship between our two companies.
